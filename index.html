Welcome to JPCollins

Financial education is not a privilege. It is a right.

JPCollins Financial Literacy Organisation is dedicated to equipping the next generation of Nigerians and Africans with the financial knowledge, skills, and confidence required to build economically independent and sustainable futures.

Our programmes serve teenagers aged 14 to 19, young professionals aged 20 to 35, and women entrepreneurs — delivering evidence-based financial education across Nigeria.

We are teaching it as a self-defense mechanism against a system eager to monetize their ignorance.

About JPCollins

Closing the financial education gap across Nigeria.

JPCollins was established in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria, with a clear mandate: to close the financial education gap that leaves millions of young Nigerians vulnerable to debt, poverty, and economic exclusion.

Since our founding, we have delivered in-person financial literacy workshops across five schools in three Nigerian states — reaching over 500 students with practical, life-changing financial education. Our work spans credit counselling, budgeting, investment literacy, entrepreneurship, and climate credit awareness.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Africa's foremost financial literacy institution

To establish JPCollins as Africa's foremost financial literacy institution — delivering structured, practical, and transformative financial education to every teenager, young professional, and woman across the continent. We are committed to fostering financially literate generations capable of building sustainable, independent, and prosperous futures.

Our Mission

Empower through practical financial education

The mission of JPCollins is to empower teenagers, youth, and women through comprehensive, practical financial education — equipping each individual with the knowledge to manage credit responsibly, avoid predatory debt, build sustainable income, and develop the entrepreneurial mindset required for long-term financial independence.

Core Values

What we stand for

The principles that guide every workshop, every counselling session, and every partnership.

Financial Mastery & Credit Strategy

We deliver comprehensive workshops on budgeting, saving, and strategic debt elimination. Through personalised credit counselling, we guide individuals away from predatory lending and toward elite credit management.

Evidence-Based Education

Every curriculum is grounded in research and real-world application — ensuring the knowledge we share translates into lasting behaviour change and measurable outcomes.

Inclusion & Access

Financial education is a right, not a privilege. We reach teenagers, young professionals, and women entrepreneurs who are most vulnerable to economic exclusion.

Long-Term Thinking

We don't teach quick fixes. We build the habits, mindset, and discipline that compound into generational wealth and sustainable prosperity.

Integrity & Trust

We operate transparently and ethically — earning the trust of schools, partners, and the communities we serve with every interaction.

Entrepreneurial Mindset

We transform passive earners into wealth creators — instilling the confidence and strategic thinking needed to build sustainable income.
