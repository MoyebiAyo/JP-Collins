Quick answers

Frequently asked questions

Who can join the programme?

Our programme serves teenagers (14–19), young professionals (20–35), and women entrepreneurs across Nigeria.

How long is the programme?

The core Financial Literacy Programme runs for six weeks, covering budgeting, credit, investing, and more.

Can schools partner with JPCollins?

Yes! We deliver in-person workshops in schools across Nigeria. Reach out through the form above to discuss a partnership.

Do participants receive a certificate?

Yes. Students who complete the programme earn a Certificate of Financial Literacy. Learn more →
